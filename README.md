# 🏠 Real Estate Sales Performance Dashboard  

## 📌 End-to-End Business Intelligence & Performance Analytics Project  

A comprehensive data analytics project focused on evaluating real estate performance across sales, agents, projects, and property status. This dashboard transforms raw operational data into actionable insights to support strategic decision-making in property sales and revenue optimization.

---

## 🚀 Problem Statement  

Real estate businesses often struggle to answer:

- Which agents are driving the most revenue?  
- Are we meeting our sales targets consistently?  
- How do new property listings compare with actual sales?  
- Which projects generate the highest returns?  
- What is the current status of property inventory?  

This dashboard provides clear answers through data-driven analysis.

---

## 🎯 Project Objective  

To design an interactive dashboard that helps:

- Track overall sales vs targets  
- Monitor agent performance  
- Analyze project-level revenue contribution  
- Understand property lifecycle (vacant, occupied, unlisted)  
- Identify trends in property listings and sales

# 📈 Key Business Insights  

## 🏆 Top Performing Agent  

- A single agent contributes the highest share of total revenue  

**Insight:**  
Business performance is highly dependent on top performers.

---

## 🎯 Sales vs Target Performance  

- Several months exceed targets  
- Some months underperform  

**Insight:**  
Sales performance is inconsistent and needs better forecasting.

---

## 📅 Listings vs Sales Trend  

- New property listings fluctuate monthly  
- Sales do not always match listing trends  

**Insight:**  
More listings ≠ more sales → conversion efficiency matters.

---

## 🏗 Project Revenue Distribution  

- Few projects dominate total revenue  

**Insight:**  
Revenue concentration increases business risk.

---

## 🏠 Property Status Analysis  

- Mix of occupied, vacant, and unlisted properties  

**Insight:**  
Unused inventory = missed revenue opportunities.

---

## 🌍 Regional Performance  

- Sales vary across states/regions  

**Insight:**  
Certain regions show stronger demand and growth potential.

---

## 📊 Revenue Concentration  

- Revenue is concentrated among top agents and projects  

**Insight:**  
Performance is not evenly distributed.

---

## 💼 Strategic Recommendations  

- Reduce dependency on top-performing agents  
- Improve conversion from listings to sales  
- Focus on high-performing projects  
- Activate vacant and unlisted properties  
- Strengthen underperforming regions
